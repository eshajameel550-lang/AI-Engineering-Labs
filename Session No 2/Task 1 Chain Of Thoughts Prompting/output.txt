Problem 1: What is 15% of 200?

WITHOUT CoT
30

WITH STEP-BY-STEP
Step 1: Convert 15% into decimal: 15/100 = 0.15
Step 2: Multiply 0.15 × 200 = 30

Final Answer: 30

Problem 2: If 5 apples cost 50 rupees, what do 8 apples cost?

WITHOUT CoT
80 rupees

WITH STEP-BY-STEP
Step 1: Cost of 1 apple = 50 ÷ 5 = 10 rupees
Step 2: Cost of 8 apples = 8 × 10 = 80 rupees

Final Answer: 80 rupees

Problem 3: A square has side 6 cm. What is its area?

WITHOUT CoT
36 cm²

WITH STEP-BY-STEP
Step 1: Formula of area of a square = side × side
Step 2: 6 × 6 = 36

Final Answer: 36 cm²
